International Connections

A ministry of Sherwood Oaks Christian Church.

International Connections provides opportunities for internationals to learn about the Bible and Christianity and grow in the Christian faith. Internationals from all countries and religious backgrounds are welcome to participate in any activity.
International connections allows people from all over the world to learn about the Bible and grow in their Christian faith. People from all religious backgrounds and cultures are welcome at any of our events!

International Women's Connection

International Women's Connection (IWC) is a special group under the International Connections for international and American women to come together in friendship in order to learn together and share common interests. International Women's Connection is responsible for all the activities organized exclusively to women.
